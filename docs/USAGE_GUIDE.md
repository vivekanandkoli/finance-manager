# 📖 Complete Usage Guide - Finance Manager

## 🚀 Quick Start

### First-Time Setup (5 minutes)

#### Step 1: Set Up Your Accounts (2 min)
1. Click **💼 Accounts** in the sidebar
2. Add your primary accounts:
   - **Salary Account**: Add your main bank account with current balance
   - **Credit Cards**: Add all credit cards with credit limits
   - **Savings**: Add savings accounts, PPF accounts
   - **Cash**: Track cash in hand
   - **Wallets**: Digital wallets (Paytm, Google Pay, etc.)

**Example:**
```
Account 1:
- Type: Bank Account
- Name: HDFC Salary Account
- Balance: ₹1,50,000
- Bank: HDFC Bank
- Account: 1234

Account 2:
- Type: Credit Card
- Name: HDFC Regalia
- Available Credit: ₹1,80,000
- Credit Limit: ₹2,00,000
- Bank: HDFC Bank
```

#### Step 2: Add Your Income Sources (1 min)
1. Click **💸 Income** in the sidebar
2. Add your regular income:
   - **Monthly Salary**: Set as "Monthly" recurring
   - **Freelance Income**: As one-time or recurring
   - **Investment Returns**: Dividends, interest
   - **Other Sources**: Rental, business income

**Example:**
```
Income 1:
- Source: Monthly Salary
- Category: Salary
- Amount: ₹1,50,000
- Frequency: Monthly
- Taxable: Yes

Income 2:
- Source: Freelance Project
- Category: Freelance
- Amount: ₹50,000
- Frequency: One-time
- Taxable: Yes
```

#### Step 3: Add Fixed Deposits & Savings (2 min)
1. Click **🏦 Deposits & Savings** in the sidebar
2. Add all your long-term savings:
   - **PPF**: Public Provident Fund
   - **EPF**: Employee Provident Fund
   - **Fixed Deposits**: All FDs with maturity dates
   - **NPS**: National Pension System
   - **Other Schemes**: NSC, SCSS, etc.

**Example:**
```
Deposit 1:
- Type: PPF
- Account Name: PPF Account 2020
- Bank: SBI
- Principal: ₹5,00,000
- Current Value: ₹6,50,000
- Interest Rate: 7.1%
- Monthly Contribution: ₹12,500
- Start Date: Jan 2020
- Maturity: Jan 2035

Deposit 2:
- Type: Fixed Deposit
- Account Name: HDFC FD #1
- Bank: HDFC Bank
- Principal: ₹5,00,000
- Current Value: ₹5,20,000
- Interest Rate: 6.5%
- Start Date: Jan 2025
- Maturity: Jan 2026
```

## 🔔 Alerts & Reminders

### What to Watch For:

#### 🚨 High Priority
- **Credit Card Utilization > 70%**: Indicates high usage, may affect credit score
- **Maturity in 30 Days**: Plan FD renewal or withdrawal
- **Budget Exceeded**: Overspent in a category
- **Bill Due Soon**: Payment reminder

#### ⚠️ Medium Priority
- **Goal Milestone Reached**: 25%, 50%, 75% complete
- **Monthly Income Not Added**: Missing salary entry
- **Loan EMI Due**: Upcoming payment

#### 💡 Low Priority
- **Expense Pattern Change**: Sudden increase in category
- **Investment Returns**: Performance update
- **Currency Rate Change**: Impact on foreign transactions

## 💼 Best Practices

### Account Management
✅ **DO:**
- Update balances weekly
- Reconcile with bank statements monthly
- Track cash transactions immediately
- Use descriptive account names

❌ **DON'T:**
- Forget to update credit card balances
- Mix personal and business accounts
- Ignore small cash expenses
- Keep inactive accounts

### Income Tracking
✅ **DO:**
- Add income as soon as received
- Mark taxable/non-taxable correctly
- Set recurring for regular income
- Add description for clarity

❌ **DON'T:**
- Forget freelance income
- Miss dividend/interest income
- Ignore reimbursements
- Leave source blank

### Deposit Management
✅ **DO:**
- Update values quarterly
- Set maturity alerts
- Track monthly contributions
- Add auto-renewal preference

❌ **DON'T:**
- Forget maturity dates
- Miss interest rate changes
- Ignore premature withdrawal penalties
- Forget to track EPF/PPF

## 🎓 Pro Tips

### Tip 1: Use Multi-Currency Wisely
If you earn/spend in multiple currencies:
1. Add accounts in respective currencies
2. Use **Currency Converter** for reference
3. Let the app auto-convert for totals
4. Track exchange rate impact

### Tip 2: Credit Card Optimization
1. Keep utilization below 30%
2. Pay full amount before due date
3. Track rewards/cashback separately
4. Set alerts for high usage

### Tip 3: Tax Optimization
1. Max out 80C deductions (₹1.5L):
   - PPF: ₹1.5L/year
   - EPF: Automatic deduction
   - NPS: Additional ₹50k under 80CCD(1B)
2. Track all taxable income
3. Maintain proper records
4. Plan year-end investments

### Tip 4: Emergency Fund
1. Keep 6 months expenses in liquid funds
2. Track separately in **Accounts**
3. Don't include in regular calculations
4. Replenish if used

### Tip 5: Goal-Based Saving
1. Create specific goals in **Goal Tracker**
2. Link deposits to goals mentally
3. Review progress monthly
4. Celebrate milestones

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Common Issues:

#### "My balance doesn't match!"
**Solution:**
1. Go to **Data Manager**
2. Export all transactions
3. Compare with bank statement
4. Find missing/duplicate entries
5. Correct and re-import

#### "Credit card limit showing wrong"
**Solution:**
1. Edit credit card account
2. Update "Available Credit" = Limit - Used Amount
3. Update "Credit Limit" = Total Limit

#### "Deposit value not updating"
**Solution:**
1. Calculate manually using:
   - FD: P × (1 + r/n)^(nt)
   - PPF: Use PPF calculator
2. Update "Current Value" field
3. Set reminder for next quarter

#### "Income total seems off"
**Solution:**
1. Check period filter (This Month/Year/All)
2. Verify all entries are in same currency
3. Check if any income marked as non-taxable
4. Recalculate manually to verify

## 🎯 Success Metrics

Track these monthly to measure financial health:

### Income Metrics
- **Savings Rate**: (Income - Expenses) / Income × 100
  - Target: **20-30%**
- **Debt-to-Income**: Total Debt / Monthly Income
  - Target: **< 36%**

### Asset Metrics
- **Emergency Fund**: 6 months expenses
  - Target: **✅ Fully Funded**
- **Investment Rate**: Investments / Net Worth
  - Target: **> 60%**

### Liability Metrics
- **Credit Utilization**: Used / Limit × 100
  - Target: **< 30%**
- **Debt Payoff Timeline**: Months to be debt-free
  - Target: **Decreasing**
